border-collapse conflict resolution

1. Style precedence (same width)

Greater visual weight wins. Order: hidden > double > solid > dashed > dotted > none (with hidden as the absolute winner). Below: orange double, then green solid, then blue dashed, then red dotted.

dotted reddashed bluesolid greendouble orange

2. Width precedence (different widths)

Wider border wins regardless of style.

2px red8px blue (wins)5px green

3. border-style: hidden beats everything

Even though red and green declare 12px solid, the middle cell's 4px hidden border wipes them out at the shared edge.

12px solid red4px HIDDEN12px solid green

4. border-style: none loses to everything

Middle cell declares none; neighbors win their solid borders.

4px solid red4px NONE4px solid green

5. Tie-breaker by source

All declarations are 8px solid. Per spec the cell wins over row, rowgroup, col, colgroup and table.

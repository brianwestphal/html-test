Emphasis & text-level semantics

em — stress emphasis (italic by default).
strong — importance (bold by default).
b — keyword/no semantic emphasis (bold by default).
i — alternate voice (italic by default).
u — unarticulated annotation (underline by default).
s — no longer accurate (strike-through by default).
small — side comment, smaller font size.
mark — highlighted passage.
del — removed content. and ins — added content.

Inline combined: this is very emphatically important information, accompanied by a brief side comment and a highlighted term.

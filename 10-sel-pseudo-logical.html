Logical pseudo-classes — :not, :is, :where, :has

:not(.skip)

  • blue
  • default (skipped)
  • blue

:is(h2, h3, h4)

h2 (green bg)

h3 (green bg)

h4 (green bg)

:where vs :is specificity

h2 stays black (plain h2 rule wins over :where)

h3 red (from :where)

:has()

  • plain item (no icon, no badge — italic grey)
  • item with image (yellow)
  • item with NEW badge (orange outline)
  • another plain (grey italic)

overscroll-behavior

When you scroll to the top or bottom of each panel, the browser's chain behavior differs:

  • auto — scrolling continues to the parent (whole page).
  • contain — scroll is contained inside; no parent scroll or pull-to-refresh.
  • none — same as contain, and also blocks browser overscroll glow/bounce effects.

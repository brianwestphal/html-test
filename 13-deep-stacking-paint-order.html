Stacking paint order — seven levels

Within a single stacking context the painting order from back to front is: (1) the box's own background & borders, (2) descendants with negative z-index (deeper first), (3) in-flow non-inline non-positioned descendants, (4) non-positioned floats, (5) in-flow inline non-positioned descendants, (6) positioned descendants with z-index: auto or 0, (7) positioned descendants with positive z-index (lower first).

2 — negative z (-1)
3 — block in-flow
4 — float
5 — inline-block
6 — pos z:auto
7 — pos z:5

Expected stack (back→front): yellow stage bg → blue (-1) → green block → orange float → purple inline-block → pink z:auto → black z:5.
